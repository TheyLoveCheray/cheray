About

We don't wear makeup to look pretty, We wear it because we enjoy the process of putting it on The "Natural Look" makes us cringe and bright colors are more fun. Makeup is not a tool meant to make an ugly thing beautiful, It's meant to only magneify the beauty that already exists. We don't do it for boys, We do it for ourselves. I wear makeup for MYSELF, because I LIKE IT. Eyeshadow is our drug, Sephora, Ultra, MAC, are our dealers. Makeup isn't just materials, its a Passion.



Makeup is a way for woman to look and feel like herself. Only prettier and more confident.
